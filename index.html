Each piece reflects my passion for painting and the lessons I've learned, including patience and emotional control. I hope to share the peace and hope it brings me with others through Ansh.

The sky in this painting is a beautiful shade of pink, reminiscent of a fairy tale. It inspires feelings of freedom and a desire to escape into a world of pureness and simplicity. The image depicts a world without the struggles and difficulties we face in our daily lives, reminding us to seek the easy path in life rather than the more challenging one.


This painting features an owl, an animal known for not sleeping at night. The owl was included during a time when the i was experiencing difficulty sleeping. The painting serves as a reminder that time passes and new days bring hope. In the meantime, we can admire the moon and stars on chilly nights and find solace in their beauty.


I did not put any deliberate effort into this piece, instead allowing my child to express his creativity freely. This painting serves as a reminder that sometimes in life, we don't need to put in effort to create something beautiful. The perspective we have on life can change greatly depending on our viewpoint. For example, mountains may appear small from a distance but become larger as we approach them, while from the mountain's perspective, we may appear small but our lives are still full of meaning and purpose.


This painting is a classic scene featuring an ocean, boat, sunset, coconut trees, birds, and clouds. As a child, we have all likely drawn a similar picture, and the artist finally got the chance to bring this vision to life as an adult. The inclusion of the boat and the birds add a touch of life to the serene scene, while the clouds add a playful element to the beautiful sunset.


This painting depicts a chilly day by a riverbank. The sound of water flowing and colliding with stones creates a peaceful atmosphere, while iam sitting on a stone with my legs in the water. The water is soft and cold, and the overall scene invokes a feeling of calmness and serenity. I want to stay in this moment, surrounded by the sounds of nature, for an infinite amount of time, lost in thought and free from worries.
